01 — The Concept

What is Norenchise?

A partnership model that bridges the gap between wholesale and franchise — built on trust, mutual success, and the Japanese tradition of 暖簾分け.

The Challenge

Traditional models force a trade-off:

  • Wholesale — Low overhead for HQ, but minimal profit per client
  • Franchise — High profit potential, but requires massive infrastructure investment

Norenchise captures the upside of both without the downsides.

Core Principles

Partnership
Not vendor-client or franchisor-franchisee. True partners with aligned incentives.
Trust-Based
No rigid audits or compliance penalties. Quality through relationship.
Full Ecosystem
Partners buy our complete product suite — beans, cakes, cookies, cups, retail.
Low Royalty
2.5% on Tokyo Coffee products only. Partner's other ventures are theirs.

The Break-Even Problem

To Break Even on Franchise Overhead

With 7% royalty: Need ¥98M - ¥119M in franchisee revenue
That's approximately 4-5 franchise locations at ¥24M/year each

The problem: We'd need to invest ¥7M+ BEFORE our first franchisee, then charge high fees to recover costs — making us uncompetitive and creating an adversarial relationship.

Norenchise Eliminates This Overhead
