The Buyer Psychology Framework

Amazon shoppers follow a predictable pattern when evaluating coffee products. Your images must address each stage sequentially:

  • Desire (Images 4-5): How will this taste/feel? Create sensory connection through lifestyle imagery and flavor visualization.

Mobile-First Consideration

Over 60% of Amazon traffic is mobile. Images 2-4 are most critical as they appear without scrolling. Ensure all text is legible at minimum 24pt equivalent, and critical information occupies the center 70% of each image (edges get cropped on some devices).

Infographic Design Guidelines

  • Visual Hierarchy: Most important information should be largest and most prominent. Use 3 levels maximum: headline, subhead, body text.
  • Color Consistency: Establish a brand color palette and use it across all infographics. Suggest: Coffee brown (#8B4513), Cream (#F5E6D3), Forest green (#2E7D32) for organic.
  • Icon Usage: Use simple, recognizable icons to represent features. Avoid overly detailed illustrations that don't read at small sizes.
  • Whitespace: Don't overcrowd. Each infographic should communicate 3-4 key points maximum.
  • Typography: Use 1-2 fonts maximum. Sans-serif for readability at small sizes. Avoid script or decorative fonts.

04 ‚Äî Messaging Strategy

What to Say (and Not Say)

Effective infographic copy is ruthlessly concise. Every word must earn its place. Here's the exact messaging framework for Tokyo Coffee images.

Words to Use

  • Authenticity Signals: "Traditional," "Small-batch," "Hand-picked," "Artisan," "Family-owned"
  • Quality Signals: "Certified," "Organic," "Premium," "100% Arabica," "Specialty"
  • Origin Signals: "Tokyo," "Japan," "Japanese," "Roastery," "Crafted"
  • Sensory Words: "Smooth," "Rich," "Sweet," "Balanced," "Aromatic"

Words to Avoid

  • Generic Claims: "Best," "Amazing," "Incredible," "Perfect" (unsubstantiated superlatives)
  • Price References: Never mention price, discounts, or value in images
  • Competitor Mentions: Amazon prohibits comparative claims
  • Health Claims: Avoid "healthy," "improves energy," etc. (FDA regulated)

Amazon Compliance Note

Images cannot contain: promotional text ("Sale," "Best Seller"), watermarks/logos outside the product, website URLs, or any claims that cannot be substantiated. All certifications shown must be verifiable.
