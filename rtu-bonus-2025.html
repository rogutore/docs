02 â€” Core Principles

The Non-Negotiables

Before determining any bonus amounts, we anchor to three principles that override all other considerations.

THE THREE NON-NEGOTIABLES

1. Cash flow is king. You cannot spend money you don't have. Paper profits mean nothing if you can't make payroll.

2. Maintain borrowing capacity. Build and maintain a balance sheet that allows RTU to borrow whenever growth opportunities arise.

3. Long-term growth over short-term reward. Sustainability first. The company must be stronger after bonuses, not weaker.

Why Borrowing Capacity Matters

RTU is a growth company. The renovation, roastery expansion, new productsâ€”all require capital. The goal isn't to borrow now. The goal is to always be able to borrow when opportunities arise.
