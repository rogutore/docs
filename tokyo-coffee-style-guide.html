Clear Space & Minimum Size

Clear Space Rule

Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "O" (coffee bean) on all sides. This ensures the logo remains legible and impactful in all contexts.

  • Minimum width (digital): 120px to maintain legibility
  • Minimum width (print): 30mm for clear reproduction
  • Clear space: Equal to the height of the coffee bean element

Logo Don'ts

✓ Do

  • Use official color combinations
  • Maintain clear space
  • Scale proportionally
  • Use on approved backgrounds

✗ Don't

  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Change the colors
  • Add effects (shadows, outlines)
  • Place on busy backgrounds
  • Rotate the logo
04 — Voice & Messaging

Brand Voice

Tokyo Coffee's voice is warm, confident, and knowledgeable. We speak with the expertise of passionate roasters while remaining approachable and welcoming to all coffee lovers.

Key Messages

  • Lead with: "東京発の人気オーガニックコーヒーロースター" — Popular Tokyo organic coffee roaster
  • Emphasize: 有機JAS認証, 100%オーガニック, 自家焙煎 — Organic certification, in-house roasting
  • Close with: "Amazonオーガニックコーヒー販売数No.1" — #1 Amazon organic coffee sales

Tagline

"体にも地球にも優しい"
Good for body and earth

Messaging Do's and Don'ts

✓ Say

  • "東京発の人気オーガニックコーヒーロースター"
  • "体にも地球にも優しい"
  • "有機JAS認証取得"
  • "新鮮な焙煎"

✗ Avoid

  • "お届けします" (too generic)
  • "立川" in national marketing (too local)
  • "ロースタリー" alone (prefer "人気ロースター")
05 — Photography Style

Visual Style

Photography should feel warm, natural, and inviting — capturing the essence of quality craft coffee and the welcoming atmosphere of our cafés.

Photography Guidelines

  • Lighting: Natural light preferred, soft and warm. Avoid harsh shadows or overly cool tones.
  • Composition: Clean, uncluttered backgrounds. Focus on the product or experience.
  • Textures: Incorporate warm wood, greenery, natural materials to reinforce organic values.
  • Depth of field: Soft bokeh backgrounds to create focus and warmth.
  • Color grading: Warm tones, slightly desaturated for a calm, premium feel.

Subject Matter

✓ Include

  • Coffee beans (whole and ground)
  • Brewing processes and equipment
  • Café interiors with warm ambiance
  • Lifestyle moments — people enjoying coffee
  • Origin imagery — farms, landscapes
  • Product packaging in context

✗ Avoid

  • Stock photo aesthetics
  • Cold, clinical lighting
  • Overly busy or cluttered scenes
  • Heavy filters or effects
  • Generic coffee imagery

AI Image Generation

When using AI tools (ImageFX, Photoshop, etc.) to generate lifestyle imagery, always incorporate these elements: natural lighting, warm wood textures, greenery/plants, clean backgrounds, and the Tokyo Coffee packaging or products as focal points.
