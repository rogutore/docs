01 — The Opportunity

Why This Matters

We're currently missing relationships with some of Japan's most established coffee importers. These three suppliers represent a significant upgrade in sourcing capability, quality, and potentially margins.

石光商事 → ユーエスフーズ
Japan's Largest Coffee Importer

石光商事 (TSE Standard: 2750) is Japan's #1 coffee importer. Their subsidiary ユーエスフーズ offers small-lot B2B purchasing with full JAS certification. We have no relationship with this market leader — that's a gap we need to close.

小川珈琲
Triple-Certified Industry Pioneer

Kyoto-based since 1952. First in Japan with Bird Friendly certification (2005). Triple certified: 有機JAS (2001) + Fairtrade (2003) + Bird Friendly. Their 業務用オンラインショップ launched Sept 2024 with 30+ green bean varieties.

ボルカフェ (Volcafe)
Global Giant, 100+ Year History

Swiss parent (1851), Japan office since 1919. ED&F Man Group subsidiary. Direct relationships with 43,000 farmers across 10 countries. Their Volcafe Way program and プロジェクトZERO defect standards are industry-leading.

JAS Compliance Note

JAS表示 is only permitted on 原袋 (30kg/60kg/69kg) purchases. Small-lot purchases can only be labeled "有機JAS認証生豆を原材料に使用" without the JAS mark. Tokyo Coffee's existing JAS certification allows 原袋購入→自社焙煎→JASマーク表示 workflow.
